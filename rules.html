RULES

Violation of these rules will result in punishment, the severity of which is determined by a staff member. The punishment will be based on the seriousness of the offense and the player’s history of violations. Punishments can range from a verbal warning to a temporary or permanent mute or ban.

These rules apply to all users except the Owner.

1) Account rules:

This rules apply to your account on the server.
  • 1.1 - Nickname must be appropriate. Incorrect ones include: immoral content (the name of the genitals, processes, etc.), has forbidden/swear words or offends another person/player.
  • 1.2 - Your skin must be appropriate. Inappropriate skins include, but are not limited to: those that are sexually explicit, depict nudity, or contain offensive symbols.
  • 1.3 - It is forbidden to use nicknames or skins that impersonate staff members, YouTubers, or other players.
  • 1.4 - The sale, transfer, exchange, or unauthorized access (hacking) of accounts is strictly prohibited.
  • 1.5 - It is forbidden to create more than six(6) accounts per IP address.
  • 1.5.1 - No more than six(6) accounts may be played simultaneously from a single IP address.
  • 1.6 - Using alternate accounts to bypass a punishment on your main account is strictly forbidden and will result in a permanent ban on all associated accounts.
  • 1.7 - Alternate accounts may not be used to gain an unfair gameplay advantage, such as for AFK farming, boosting stats, or circumventing server limitations.
  • 1.8 - You are solely responsible for the security of your account. Punishments will be applied to the account on which the violation occurred, regardless of who was playing at the time.

  • 2.7 - Immoral behavior prohibited, topics for adults.
  • 2.8 - Out-of-game deception of players. For example, by keyboard shortcuts Alt+F4, commands, and so on.
  • 2.9 - Inappropriate behavior, excessive insults and aggression.
  • 2.10 - Public criticism of the project in chat is prohibited.
  • 2.10.1 - It is prohibited to humiliate/insult the project, offensive names/words, etc.
